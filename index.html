Emergency
CASI : E-PANIC BUTTON

CASI is a e-panic button application that has all emergency services such as police, private
security personnel, EMS and Fire department operating under one command centre,
making it easy and convenient to get emergency help with just a click of a button when you
need it.

Ebhodo Holdings has the distribution rights of CASI.

We’re proud to announce that our current client is the Department of Community Safety
Gauteng.

With more than 10 000 downloads and more than 4000 customers using or e panic button.
